Kit Information

Frog Kit:

The Frog Kit has 5 power ups. Drop the power up in your inventory to super jump!

Fighter Kit:

The Fighter Kit has 5 power ups. Drop the power up in your inventory to gain speed 5 for 5 seconds!

Ninja Kit:

The Ninja Kit has 7 ninja stars. Drop the ninja stars in your inventory to damage people in a 2 block radius!

Ghost Kit:

The Ghost Kit has 5 power ups. Drop the power up to gain invisibility for 7 seconds!

Ant Man Kit:

The Ant Man Kit has 3 unlimited power ups. Drop one of the power ups to shrink, default, or grow yourself!

Barbarian Kit:

The Barbarian Kit has 5 power ups. Drop the power up to gain invincibility for 5 seconds!

Poseidon Kit:

The Poseidon Kit has no power ups. You heal in water, have a trident, and depth strider 5!

Air Striker Kit:

The Air Striker Kit has 3 air strikes. Drop the air strike to summon an air strike!

Speed Kit:

The Speed Kit has no power ups. You have the effect speed 2!

Viking Kit:

The Viking Kit has no power ups. You have a viking battle axe with sharpness 5 and knockback 5!

Poisoner Kit:

The Poisoner Kit has 5 poison bombs. Drop the poison bombs to damage all entities in a radius with poison!

UHC Kit:

The UHC Kit has no power ups. You have a shield, an iron axe, and 15 golden apples!

Giant Kit:

The Giant Kit has no power ups. You get mining fatigue 4, slowness 3, strength 3, and protection 6!
